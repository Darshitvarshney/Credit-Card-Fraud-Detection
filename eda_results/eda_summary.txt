=== Credit Card Default Dataset: EDA Summary ===

1. Target variable is imbalanced (more non-defaults).
2. Repayment history (PAY_1 … PAY_6) shows strong link with default.
3. EDUCATION and MARRIAGE categories had invalid values; fixed.
4. Bill amounts and payment amounts are correlated with default risk.
5. Demographics (SEX, AGE) show weaker influence.
